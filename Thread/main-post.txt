[size=6][b]ALL STEAMPUNK ITEMS[/b][/size]
[img]https://64.media.tumblr.com/ce55d7132ec2756b62b0ade2aa14f6ad/b97b32028fcfb246-aa/s250x400/58b527724338c849e34a401de97aa410f2332c44.png[/img][/center]

I quite enjoy the steampunk aesthetic (at this point I have a [url=https://www1.flightrising.com/den/676173/2460600]den tab[/url] for dragons based on it), but have been having a hard time remembering which apparel sets and scenes go well with it. So I started making an informal list, primarily for my own convenience, and decided to turn it into a proper public resource, encompassing all categories of items, after coming across [url=https://www1.flightrising.com/clan-profile/349527]Tidepools[/url]' [url=https://www1.flightrising.com/forums/gde/3171589]All Aquatic Items[/url] thread.

This list is likely not comprehensive, and purely based on my own perception of what classifies as "steampunk" – that said, you are absolutely welcome to comment in this thread (or send me a PM) if you disagree with the inclusion of an item, or want to suggest an item to be added. There's a good chance I just missed some things while going through the database. [emoji=new friend size=1]
(Do note that for skins I'm currently only including site skins in the list, since I don't know how to get even close to a comprehensive list of UMAs that fit this aesthetic.)
