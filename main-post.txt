[i](The following paragraph is not going to stay (in this form), it's mostly just a note to myself about how I've been choosing what to include.)[/i]
This has been surprisingly difficult to make. Steampunk feels a lot less precisely defined than aquatic items, for example. And I'm very much using my own interpretation of the aesthetic, which leads to my choices veering too far into light academia and other similar aesthetics I also like, I think. I've also been valuing the colour scheme a lot, which leads to some mechanical things being excluded because they're not primarily brown/beige/gold/copper, and some non-mechanical items being included in my pre-selections because they are. Steampunk is also closely related to the Lightning flight on this website specifically, but far from every Lightning-related item is also steampunk. (In my opinion, anyway.) I've also been treating items with yellow or white accents as "more steampunk" than ones with blue or green ones, for example. But I'm not sure if that is actually part of the aesthetic, or just personal bias.
I'll have to do a lot of deciding to finish this thread....
